About

A very fortunate & grateful amateur equine photographer sharing her magical journey with the wider world. I love to photography our friends in wide open spaces to capture their personality and character. I first got interested in photography many years ago and later came my love and interest in horses so what better fit.
I hope you enjoy my shots from the various countries I have had the fortunate opportunity to visit and look forward to many more equine photography trips both locally and internationally.
